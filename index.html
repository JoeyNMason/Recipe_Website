Recipe of the Week

Bangin' BLT

Ingriedents

  • Bacon
  • 1 head of Lettuce
  • 1 Tomato
  • Loaf of White Bread or Seeded Bread
  • Mayonaise
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Chilli Flakes(Optional)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Lay 4 strips of bacon into a cold frying pan
  2. Put the frying pan on the hob and to GasMark 5
  3. No oil or butter is required, once the pan heats up the fat will start to render cooking the bacon in its own fat
  4. Wash the vegtables
  5. Cut head of lettuce in half
  6. Take 1/2 of the lettuce and shred the lettuce
  7. Slice the tomato the lay out the slices
  8. Lightly salt and pepper both sides of the tomato slices
  9. Keep watch on the bacon, once one side starts sizziling flip the bacon
  10. For the perfect BLT, white bread or seeded bread is a must
  11. Toast two slices of bread
  12. Butter the two slices(optional)
  13. Spread Mayonaise on both slices of bread, seasoning with chilli flakes is optional
  14. At this point the bacon will be done, of course cook to the desired texture you desire
  15. Start by layering one slice of bread with a bed of lettuce
  16. Add the tomato on top, making sure the tomato slices are seasoned
  17. Layer your rashes of bacon on top
  18. Top with the seconed slice of bread
  19. And voila the perfect BLT is finished
